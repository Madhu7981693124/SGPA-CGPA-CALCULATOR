ℹ️ About This App

This SGPA & CGPA Calculator is designed for students following the R23 Regulation. It helps you calculate your Semester Grade Point Average (SGPA) and Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) easily.

Features:

  • Calculate SGPA for individual semesters
  • Track CGPA across all semesters
  • Save and manage your academic data
  • Visual charts for performance trends
  • Dark mode support
